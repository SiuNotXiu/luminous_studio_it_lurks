Dirt Footstep:      When player is walking on dirt/dirt road
Grass Footstep:     When player is walking on grass
Cornfield Footstep: When the player is walking in cornfield
Concrete Footstep:  When the player is walking on concrete/stone
Wood Footstep:      When the player is walking on wooden floors
Flashlight:         When the player turns on/off their flashlight
Flashlight Flicker: When the player's flashlight is about to die
Heartbeat:          When the player is one hit away from dying
